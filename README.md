# datacentrecomments

## What is this

An analysis of public comments submitted to four UK planning applications for data centres in Wiltshire and North Lincolnshire. 807 comments. 756 of them contained actual words rather than the phrase "please see attached."

It turns out a lot of people have a lot of feelings about data centres. I have now read all of them.

---

## Why does this exist

I was passing through Wiltshire, overheard a heated local conversation about a planning application, and instead of doing the normal thing — continuing to drive, forgetting about it, getting on with my life — I went home and spent several weeks reading planning portal comments and committee minutes.

I am not a planning expert. This is, in retrospect, obvious.

## Methodology

I read 807 planning comments. I classified them by concern category, checked for template language, cross-referenced primary planning documents, and tried very hard not to state things I couldn't support.

The bit about trying hard not to state things I couldn't support required several edits.

**Important caveat:** Four applications is not a national dataset. Please do not cite this as evidence of anything national. It is evidence of what happened in Wiltshire and North Lincolnshire, to these four applications, during this period.
